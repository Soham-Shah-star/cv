HER(Health Emergency Rescue)

HER (Health Emergency Rescue) is an innovative project designed to enhance access to medical facilities during emergencies. This application connects users to the nearest hospitals, clinics, and blood banks, providing real-time availability information. By leveraging GPS and integrated databases, HER ensures that users can quickly identify the closest medical resources, making it easier to respond to urgent health situations.

The application not only displays the availability of these facilities but also allows users to book appointments directly through the app. This feature streamlines the process of securing medical assistance, reducing wait times and improving overall efficiency in critical scenarios. Users can access up-to-date information on the status of various medical facilities, ensuring they make informed decisions in times of need.

HER aims to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers, facilitating prompt and effective medical care. With its user-friendly interface and comprehensive connectivity, HER empowers individuals to take control of their health emergencies, providing a reliable and efficient solution for accessing medical support when it matters most.

Pharmacy Genie


Pharmacy Genie is an innovative medicine dispenser designed to revolutionize the way patients manage their medication. By integrating with a user-friendly mobile app, Pharmacy Genie ensures that patients receive the correct medicine based on their diagnosis. The device streamlines the medication process, making it both efficient and reliable, thereby reducing the risk of human error in administering prescriptions.

The mobile app connected to Pharmacy Genie plays a crucial role in the diagnosis and dispensing process. Users can input their symptoms or medical conditions, and the app, using advanced algorithms and a comprehensive medical database, determines the appropriate medication. Once the diagnosis is confirmed, the app communicates with the Pharmacy Genie dispenser to release the exact dosage required. This seamless integration between the app and the dispenser ensures that patients receive their medication promptly and accurately.

Pharmacy Genie is not just a convenience tool; it also enhances patient safety and adherence to prescribed treatments. The device can store multiple types of medications and is equipped with features that remind patients when it is time to take their medicine. This is particularly beneficial for individuals with chronic conditions who require regular medication. By ensuring timely and correct dispensing of medicines, Pharmacy Genie supports better health outcomes and contributes to a more efficient healthcare system.

Paralympics Wheelchair


The Paralympics Wheelchair is a groundbreaking innovation designed to enable athletes with disabilities to actively participate in football. This specially engineered wheelchair is built to provide optimal support, agility, and control, allowing users to maneuver effortlessly on the football field. By incorporating advanced technology and robust materials, the Paralympics Wheelchair ensures that athletes can perform at their best, experiencing the thrill of the game without limitations.

Equipped with features tailored specifically for football, the Paralympics Wheelchair includes a reinforced frame, responsive wheels, and adaptive controls that cater to the unique needs of each player. A handle near the legs enables the wheelchair to kick the ball, enhancing the player's ability to interact with the game dynamically. Additionally, the wheelchair is controlled by a joystick, offering intuitive navigation and precise control, essential for quick turns and strategic plays on the field.

Beyond its technical capabilities, the Paralympics Wheelchair represents a significant step forward in promoting inclusivity in sports. By enabling athletes with disabilities to play football competitively, this wheelchair fosters a sense of empowerment and community. It highlights the potential of adaptive sports equipment to break down barriers, creating opportunities for athletes to showcase their skills and passion for the game. The Paralympics Wheelchair is more than just a piece of equipment; it is a symbol of progress and a testament to the spirit of perseverance and determination.
